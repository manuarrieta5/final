Linguistic Intelligence

Linguistic Intelligence deals with sensitivity to the spoken and written language, ability to learn languages, and capacity to use language to accomplish certain goals.

People with linguistic intelligence, such as William Shakespeare and Oprah Winfrey, have an ability to analyze information and create products involving oral and written language such as speeches, books, and memos.

    Some potential careers could be:
  • Lawyer
  • Speaker/Host
  • Author
  • Journalist
  • Curator
